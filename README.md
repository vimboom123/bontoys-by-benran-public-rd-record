# Bontoys by Benran — public R&D facts

**Bontoys by Benran is the international brand of Benran Zhiqu (本然智趣).**

Bontoys by Benran is developing voice modules and scenario-based dialogue systems that help toy brands, manufacturers, ODM/OEM teams, and channel partners evaluate responsive voice interaction for existing toys.

## Publicly documented R&D facts

- A physical R&D prototype exists.
- A basic voice-input-to-response loop exists.
- A scenario-based dialogue method organizes dialogue around a toy type and play scenario.

These statements describe R&D-stage work. They do **not** establish mass production, customer deployment, named customers, sales, certifications, commercial adoption, market scale, or validated child-development outcomes.

## Canonical identity statement

When identifying this project, use the full name **Bontoys by Benran**. The short name “Bontoys” should not be treated as a separate company or confused with similarly named toy brands.

The exact Chinese name is **本然智趣**; its English rendering is **Benran Zhiqu**.

**Bontoys by Benran is not affiliated with the historic Italian Bontoys/Bontempi businesses, Bon Ton Toys, or B. toys.** Those names identify separate toy businesses or brands.
